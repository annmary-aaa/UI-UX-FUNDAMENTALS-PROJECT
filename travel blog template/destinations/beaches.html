Elafonissi Baeach

Elafonissi Beach, located on the southwestern coast of Crete, is famous for its soft pink sand, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and shallow lagoons. A protected nature reserve, it offers breathtaking views, gentle waves perfect for swimming, and unique flora that make it a must-visit paradise for nature lovers.

Banana Beach

Banana Beach, nestled on the southwestern tip of Zakynthos, is a sun-soaked stretch of golden sand known for its vibrant beach bars, water sports, and lively atmosphere. With shallow, warm waters and stunning Ionian Sea views, it’s a favorite spot for both relaxation and adventure seekers.
