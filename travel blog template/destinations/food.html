Turkish cuisine is a flavorful fusion of Middle Eastern, Mediterranean, and Central Asian influences, known for its rich spices, fresh ingredients, and diverse dishes like kebabs, mezes, baklava, and Turkish tea. It’s a celebration of taste, tradition, and hospitality.
